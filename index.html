teddy bear dog. This teddy bear dog breed has a much shorter snout hence it is commonly referred to as smooshed face. As a result of this feature, the eyes of the Pomeranium appear to be closer to the nose and the cheeks appear to be fuller. Weighing less than 7 pounds, these teddy bear dog breeds are very small in size but have a big personality.
